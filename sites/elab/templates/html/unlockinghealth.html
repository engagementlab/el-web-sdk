Welcome to Zariba!

You are an international consultant charged with creating a program proposal fo the people of Zariba that will improve health care and raise key indicators specified by the Ministry of Health.

Find your way around the country and talk to local people to get a nuanced picture of the situation from a wide array of perspectives. Set health priorities with government ministry officials, then help implement, monitor, and adapt the plan in context as operational obstacles inevitably arise.

Strive to meet target health indicator goals over the course of three years in the game and unlock health for the people of Zariba!

Unlocking Health was commissioned by the Open Learning Campus with financial support from the Government of Korea.

This game was developed in collaboration with the Health, Nutrition and Population Global Practice of the World Bank

and was designed and created by the Engagement Lab at Emerson College. Originally released in 2016.
